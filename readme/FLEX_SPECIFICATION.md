IronRainbow Flex.

An app for managing customers, orders, jobs, so overall managing a "poweder coating factory".

Data:

- Clients
- Orders
- Jobs 
- Parts
- Blueprints
- Colors 
- Services
- Recipes
- Processes

What does the app do.

1. Handling orders, separating into jobs.
2. Job is a look with service/services and a part/part package.
3. Service is one more recipes
4. Service decides recipe based on properties set.
5. Recipes are one or more processes.
6. Processes are using machines and storage.
