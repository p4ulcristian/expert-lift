# Service Area Management

\## Dashboard

\### Overview

\- Total number of service areas (zip codes)

\- Total population served

\- Total businesses served

\- Overall profitability across all areas

\### Quick Actions

\- Add New Service Area

\- Generate Area Report

\- View Underserved Areas

\### Key Performance Indicators

\- Average profit per service area

\- Customer density (customers per square mile)

\- Market penetration rate

\- Growth rate by area

\## Service Area List

\### Searchable and Filterable Table

\- Columns: Zip Code, City, State, Population, Number of Customers,
Profitability, Growth Rate

\### Map View

\- Interactive map showing all service areas

\- Color-coded based on profitability or other selected metrics

\## Service Area Details

\### General Information

\- Zip Code

\- City and State

\- Population

\- Area (square miles)

\- Number of residential customers

\- Number of business customers

\### Economic Indicators

\- Median household income

\- Major industries

\- Economic growth rate

\### Profitability Analysis

\- Overall profitability for the area

\- Profitability by service type

\- Profitability by customer segment (residential vs. business)

\- Profit per capita

\### Customer Base

\- Total number of customers

\- Customer acquisition rate

\- Customer retention rate

\- Market share

\### Service Performance

\- Most popular services in the area

\- Average order value

\- Order frequency

\- Customer satisfaction rating

\### Operational Metrics

\- Average travel time to area

\- Delivery efficiency

\- Local partnerships or facilities

\## Item-Specific Analysis

\### Profitability by Item

\- Table showing all items/services offered

\- Columns: Item Name, Sales Volume, Revenue, Cost, Profit Margin

\- Sortable and filterable

\### Item Performance Trends

\- Historical data on item performance in the area

\- Seasonal trends

\- Price sensitivity analysis

\## Competitive Analysis

\### Local Competitors

\- List of competitors in the area

\- Estimated market share

\- Competitor strengths and weaknesses

\### Market Positioning

\- SWOT analysis for the service area

\- Opportunities for service expansion or improvement

\## Marketing and Growth

\### Marketing Campaigns

\- Current and past marketing efforts in the area

\- Campaign performance metrics

\- ROI on marketing spend

\### Growth Opportunities

\- Underserved segments or niches

\- Potential new services based on local demand

\- Partnership opportunities

\## Regulatory and Compliance

\### Local Regulations

\- Relevant local laws and regulations

\- Compliance requirements

\- Any area-specific certifications needed

\### Environmental Considerations

\- Local environmental regulations

\- Sustainability initiatives

\## Reporting and Analytics

\### Custom Report Builder

\- Select metrics and areas for comparison

\- Generate multi-area reports

\### Predictive Analytics

\- Growth forecasts for the area

\- Demand prediction for services

\- Risk assessment

\## Settings

\### Area Configuration

\- Add or remove zip codes from service area

\- Set service boundaries

\- Configure area-specific pricing

\### Data Sources

\- Manage integrations with demographic data providers

\- Configure update frequency for economic indicators

\### User Permissions

\- Set access levels for viewing and editing area data

\- Role-based permissions for sensitive financial data

\### Notification Preferences

\- Alert settings for significant changes in area performance

\- Scheduled reports for area managers
