# Marketing options

General Marketing Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Marketing Overview\*\*

\- Active campaigns

\- Lead generation metrics

\- Customer acquisition cost

\- ROI of marketing efforts

\- Upcoming scheduled activities

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Create New Campaign

\- Schedule Marketing Activity

\- Generate Marketing Report

3\. \*\*Performance Snapshot\*\*

\- Website traffic

\- Social media engagement

\- Email marketing metrics

\- Conversion rates

\## Campaign Management

1\. \*\*Campaign List\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable table with columns:

\- Campaign Name

\- Type (Email, Social Media, PPC, Event, etc.)

\- Start/End Dates

\- Status

\- Budget

\- ROI

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Duplicate, Pause/Resume

2\. \*\*Campaign Creator/Editor\*\*

\- Campaign Name and Description

\- Objectives and KPIs

\- Target Audience

\- Budget Allocation

\- Timeline and Milestones

\- Associated Offers

\- Channel Selection (Multi-channel support)

3\. \*\*Campaign Performance Tracking\*\*

\- Real-time metrics dashboard

\- A/B testing results

\- Conversion funnel analysis

\## Customer Segmentation

1\. \*\*Segment Builder\*\*

\- Create segments based on demographics, behavior, industry, etc.

\- Save and manage customer segments

2\. \*\*Segment Analysis\*\*

\- Size and composition of each segment

\- Segment performance metrics

\- Recommended marketing strategies per segment

\## Content Management

1\. \*\*Content Calendar\*\*

\- Schedule and manage marketing content across channels

2\. \*\*Content Repository\*\*

\- Store and organize marketing assets (images, videos, copy)

\- Version control and approval workflow

3\. \*\*Content Performance Tracking\*\*

\- Engagement metrics for different types of content

\## Email Marketing

1\. \*\*Email Campaign Creator\*\*

\- Design templates

\- Segment targeting

\- A/B testing setup

2\. \*\*Automation Workflows\*\*

\- Set up triggered email sequences

\- Drip campaigns

3\. \*\*Performance Analytics\*\*

\- Open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates

\- List growth and unsubscribe rates

\## Social Media Management

1\. \*\*Social Media Calendar\*\*

\- Schedule posts across platforms

2\. \*\*Social Listening\*\*

\- Monitor brand mentions and industry trends

3\. \*\*Engagement Tracking\*\*

\- Track likes, shares, comments, and conversions

\## SEO and SEM Management

1\. \*\*Keyword Tracking\*\*

\- Monitor ranking for target keywords

2\. \*\*On-page SEO Recommendations\*\*

\- Suggestions for improving website SEO

3\. \*\*PPC Campaign Management\*\*

\- Create and manage Google Ads and other PPC campaigns

\## Event Marketing

1\. \*\*Event Planner\*\*

\- Plan and manage marketing events (trade shows, webinars, etc.)

2\. \*\*Attendee Management\*\*

\- Track registrations and attendance

3\. \*\*Post-Event Analysis\*\*

\- Measure event ROI and follow-up activities

\## Lead Management

1\. \*\*Lead Capture Forms\*\*

\- Create and manage lead capture forms

2\. \*\*Lead Scoring\*\*

\- Set up and manage lead scoring criteria

3\. \*\*Lead Nurturing Workflows\*\*

\- Design and automate lead nurturing processes

\## Analytics and Reporting

1\. \*\*Marketing Dashboard\*\*

\- Customizable dashboard with key marketing metrics

2\. \*\*ROI Calculator\*\*

\- Calculate and compare ROI across different marketing activities

3\. \*\*Attribution Modeling\*\*

\- Understand the impact of different touchpoints in the customer
journey

4\. \*\*Competitor Analysis\*\*

\- Track and compare performance against competitors

\## Integrations

1\. \*\*CRM Integration\*\*

\- Sync customer data and activities with CRM system

2\. \*\*Sales Pipeline Integration\*\*

\- Track marketing contribution to sales pipeline

3\. \*\*Website Analytics Integration\*\*

\- Pull in website performance data

4\. \*\*Social Media Platform Integrations\*\*

\- Connect and manage social media accounts

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Brand Guidelines\*\*

\- Store and manage brand guidelines for consistent messaging

2\. \*\*Budget Management\*\*

\- Set and track marketing budgets

3\. \*\*Team Collaboration Tools\*\*

\- Task assignment and project management features

4\. \*\*Approval Workflows\*\*

\- Set up approval processes for campaigns and content

This interface provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing all
aspects of marketing for the coating facility, from campaign planning to
performance analysis.

# Marketing Offers Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Offer Overview\*\*

\- Total number of active offers

\- Offers by type (e.g., discount, bundle, seasonal)

\- Performance summary (redemption rate, revenue generated)

\- Upcoming and expiring offers

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Create New Offer

\- View Active Offers

\- Generate Offer Performance Report

3\. \*\*Offer List\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable table with columns:

\- Offer ID

\- Offer Name

\- Type

\- Start Date

\- End Date

\- Status (Active, Scheduled, Expired)

\- Redemption Count

\- Conversion Rate

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Duplicate,
Deactivate/Activate

\## Create/Edit Offer Form

1\. \*\*Offer Details\*\*

\- Offer Name

\- Description

\- Offer Type (e.g., Percentage Discount, Fixed Amount Off, Buy One Get
One, Bundle)

\- Start Date and End Date

\- Applicable Services/Products

2\. \*\*Discount Configuration\*\*

\- Discount Amount or Percentage

\- Minimum Order Value (if applicable)

\- Maximum Discount Amount (if applicable)

3\. \*\*Eligibility\*\*

\- Customer Segments (e.g., New Customers, VIP Clients,
Industry-specific)

\- Geographic Restrictions

\- Usage Limit (per customer and total)

4\. \*\*Promotion Codes\*\*

\- Generate unique promo code

\- Option for multiple-use or single-use codes

5\. \*\*Display Settings\*\*

\- Visibility on website/app

\- Featured offer status

6\. \*\*Terms and Conditions\*\*

\- Detailed terms of the offer

\- Exclusions and limitations

\## Offer Details Page

1\. \*\*Offer Summary\*\*

\- All details from the Create/Edit form

\- Current status and performance metrics

2\. \*\*Performance Metrics\*\*

\- Views/Impressions

\- Redemptions

\- Conversion Rate

\- Revenue Generated

\- Average Order Value with Offer

3\. \*\*Customer Insights\*\*

\- Top customer segments using the offer

\- New vs. Returning customer usage

4\. \*\*Order Details\*\*

\- List of orders that used this offer

\- Option to view full order details

5\. \*\*A/B Testing Results\*\* (if applicable)

\- Comparison of performance between offer variants

\## Campaign Integration

1\. \*\*Associate with Marketing Campaigns\*\*

\- Link offers to specific marketing campaigns

\- Track offer performance within campaign context

2\. \*\*Channel Performance\*\*

\- Analyze offer performance across different marketing channels (email,
social media, website)

\## Reporting and Analytics

1\. \*\*Offer Performance Report\*\*

\- Compare performance of different offers

\- Track offer usage over time

\- Revenue impact analysis

2\. \*\*Customer Behavior Analysis\*\*

\- Effect of offers on customer acquisition and retention

\- Repeat purchase behavior after offer redemption

3\. \*\*ROI Calculation\*\*

\- Calculate return on investment for each offer

\- Compare cost of offer vs. revenue generated

4\. \*\*Predictive Analytics\*\*

\- Suggest optimal offer types based on historical data

\- Predict potential performance of new offers

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Offer Templates\*\*

\- Create and manage templates for commonly used offer types

2\. \*\*Automation Rules\*\*

\- Set up rules for automatically activating or deactivating offers
based on performance or date

3\. \*\*Notification Preferences\*\*

\- Set up alerts for offer performance milestones, expiring offers, etc.

4\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Configure integration with CRM, Order Management, and other relevant
systems

5\. \*\*Approval Workflow\*\*

\- Set up approval process for new offers or offers above a certain
value

This interface provides a comprehensive way to create, manage, and
analyze marketing offers, enabling data-driven decision-making for
promotional strategies.
