# Facility Status Dashboard

\## Main Overview

1\. \*\*Facility Health Score\*\*

\- Overall health rating based on all monitored parameters

\- Color-coded status (Green: Optimal, Yellow: Warning, Red: Critical)

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- View All Alerts

\- Generate Status Report

\- Initiate Emergency Protocol

3\. \*\*Key Metrics at a Glance\*\*

\- Total Energy Consumption

\- Average Facility Temperature

\- Active Production Lines

\- Air Quality Index

\## Energy Consumption Metrics

1\. \*\*Total Energy Usage\*\*

\- Real-time total consumption

\- Comparison to historical average

2\. \*\*Energy Sources Breakdown\*\*

\- Electricity

\- Natural Gas

\- Solar (if applicable)

\- Other sources

3\. \*\*Consumption by Area\*\*

\- Production Lines

\- HVAC Systems

\- Lighting

\- Office Spaces

4\. \*\*Energy Efficiency Indicators\*\*

\- Energy usage per unit produced

\- Peak demand times

5\. \*\*Cost Analysis\*\*

\- Real-time energy costs

\- Projected monthly/annual costs

\## Temperature Mapping

1\. \*\*Facility Heat Map\*\*

\- Color-coded layout of facility showing temperature variations

2\. \*\*Zone-specific Temperatures\*\*

\- Production Areas

\- Storage Areas

\- Curing Ovens

\- Office Spaces

3\. \*\*Temperature Trends\*\*

\- Historical temperature data

\- Predictive temperature modeling

4\. \*\*HVAC System Status\*\*

\- Current settings

\- Performance metrics

\## Live Camera Feeds

1\. \*\*Security Cameras\*\*

\- Exterior perimeter views

\- Entrance and exit monitoring

\- Parking lot surveillance

2\. \*\*Production Area Cameras\*\*

\- Views of each production line

\- Quality control checkpoints

3\. \*\*Infrared Cameras\*\*

\- Heat signature views of critical equipment

\- Detect potential overheating issues

4\. \*\*Time-lapse Footage\*\*

\- Option to view accelerated footage for pattern recognition

\## Environmental Monitoring

1\. \*\*Air Quality Metrics\*\*

\- Particulate matter levels

\- VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds) levels

\- Humidity levels

2\. \*\*Ventilation System Status\*\*

\- Airflow rates

\- Filter status and replacement schedules

3\. \*\*Waste Management\*\*

\- Hazardous waste levels

\- Recycling metrics

\## Production Line Status

1\. \*\*Line-by-Line Overview\*\*

\- Operational status (Running, Idle, Maintenance)

\- Current production rate

\- Efficiency metrics

2\. \*\*Equipment Health\*\*

\- Status of key machinery

\- Predictive maintenance alerts

3\. \*\*Quality Control Metrics\*\*

\- Defect rates

\- First-pass yield

\## Utility Systems

1\. \*\*Electrical System\*\*

\- Main and sub-panel status

\- Power quality metrics (voltage stability, harmonics)

2\. \*\*Water Systems\*\*

\- Water consumption rates

\- Wastewater metrics

3\. \*\*Compressed Air Systems\*\*

\- Pressure levels

\- Compressor performance

\## Safety and Compliance

1\. \*\*Emergency Systems Status\*\*

\- Fire suppression system

\- Emergency lighting

\- Alarm systems

2\. \*\*Safety Incident Tracker\*\*

\- Recent incidents or near-misses

3\. \*\*Compliance Monitoring\*\*

\- Environmental compliance status

\- Safety regulation adherence

\## Predictive Analytics

1\. \*\*Energy Usage Forecasts\*\*

\- Predicted consumption based on production schedule and weather

2\. \*\*Maintenance Predictions\*\*

\- Projected equipment failures or maintenance needs

3\. \*\*Capacity Planning\*\*

\- Suggested production scheduling based on facility status

\## Reporting and Alerts

1\. \*\*Custom Report Generator\*\*

\- Create reports on any combination of facility metrics

2\. \*\*Alert Configuration\*\*

\- Set up custom alerts for various parameters

\- Define alert thresholds and notification methods

3\. \*\*Historical Data Analysis\*\*

\- Compare current status to historical data

\- Identify long-term trends and patterns

\## Mobile Access

1\. \*\*Mobile-optimized Views\*\*

\- Access critical facility data on smartphones or tablets

2\. \*\*Push Notifications\*\*

\- Receive immediate alerts on mobile devices

\## Integration and Settings

1\. \*\*IoT Device Management\*\*

\- Add and configure new sensors or monitoring devices

2\. \*\*Data Integration\*\*

\- Connect with other systems (ERP, MES, etc.)

3\. \*\*User Permissions\*\*

\- Set access levels for different roles

4\. \*\*Display Customization\*\*

\- Personalize dashboard layouts for different users or roles

This comprehensive Facility Status Dashboard provides real-time
monitoring and analysis of all critical aspects of the coating facility,
enabling proactive management and efficient operations.
