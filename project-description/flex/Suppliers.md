# Suppliers

\#### Supplier List

\- Searchable and filterable table

\- Columns: Supplier ID, Company Name, Main Products/Services,
Relationship Duration, Performance Rating

\#### Supplier Profile

\- Basic Information (Name, Contact Details, Website)

\- Products/Services Provided

\- Contract Details and Terms

\- Performance History

\- Quality Ratings

\- Pricing Information

\- Lead Times

\- Certifications and Compliance Information

\#### Order Management

\- Purchase Order History

\- Current Open Orders

\- Order Tracking and Status Updates

\#### Quality Control

\- Incoming Inspection Results

\- Non-Conformance Reports

\- Corrective Action Requests and Responses

\#### Performance Metrics

\- On-Time Delivery Rate

\- Quality Consistency Score

\- Price Competitiveness

\- Responsiveness to Inquiries/Issues

\#### Risk Assessment

\- Supply Chain Disruption Risk

\- Financial Stability Assessment

\- Compliance Risk Evaluation

\#### Supplier Development

\- Improvement Plans

\- Training and Support Initiatives

\- Collaboration Opportunities

\## Communication Hub

\- Centralized messaging system

\- Document sharing

\- Meeting scheduler

\- Automated notifications

\## Reporting and Analytics

\- Partner performance comparisons

\- Trend analysis

\- ROI calculations for partnerships

\- Supply chain optimization insights

\## Integration Features

\- Connect with Inventory Management

\- Link to Quality Control System

\- Integrate with Financial Systems for invoicing and payments

\## Settings

\- Partner Type Configuration

\- Performance Metric Customization

\- Approval Workflows

\- Access Control and Permissions
