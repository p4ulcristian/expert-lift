# Workstations Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Workstation Overview\*\*

\- Total number of workstations

\- Operational status summary (Active, Idle, Maintenance)

\- Overall productivity metrics

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Add New Workstation

\- Assign Task

\- Generate Productivity Report

3\. \*\*Alert Center\*\*

\- Quality control issues

\- Bottleneck warnings

\- Maintenance alerts

\## Workstation List

1\. \*\*Searchable and Filterable Table\*\*

\- Columns: Workstation ID, Name, Type, Current Operator, Status,
Current Task, Efficiency Rating

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Assign Task, Report Issue

2\. \*\*Workstation Categories\*\*

\- Pre-treatment stations

\- Coating application booths

\- Inspection points

\- Packaging stations

\- Quality control stations

\## Workstation Details Page

1\. \*\*General Information\*\*

\- Workstation name and ID

\- Type and purpose

\- Location in facility layout

2\. \*\*Current Status\*\*

\- Operational status

\- Current operator(s)

\- Ongoing task details

3\. \*\*Performance Metrics\*\*

\- Productivity rate

\- Quality metrics

\- Utilization rate

4\. \*\*Equipment and Tools\*\*

\- List of associated equipment

\- Tool inventory and status

\- Consumables level

5\. \*\*Task History\*\*

\- Recent completed tasks

\- Upcoming scheduled tasks

6\. \*\*Quality Control\*\*

\- Recent quality check results

\- Common defects encountered

\- Corrective actions taken

7\. \*\*Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)\*\*

\- Step-by-step guides for tasks

\- Safety guidelines

\- Training materials

\## Task Management

1\. \*\*Task Assignment\*\*

\- Assign tasks to workstations

\- Set priorities and deadlines

2\. \*\*Task Tracking\*\*

\- Real-time progress monitoring

\- Estimated vs. actual completion time

3\. \*\*Workflow Visualization\*\*

\- Gantt chart of tasks across workstations

\- Critical path analysis

4\. \*\*Load Balancing\*\*

\- View workload distribution

\- Suggestions for optimal task allocation

\## Operator Management

1\. \*\*Operator Assignments\*\*

\- Current and scheduled operator assignments

\- Skill matrix integration for optimal assignment

2\. \*\*Performance Tracking\*\*

\- Individual operator productivity metrics

\- Quality ratings

3\. \*\*Training and Certification\*\*

\- Required skills for each workstation

\- Training schedules and completion status

\## Quality Control

1\. \*\*Inspection Checklists\*\*

\- Customizable quality control checklists

\- Digital sign-off for completed inspections

2\. \*\*Defect Tracking\*\*

\- Log and categorize defects

\- Trend analysis of common issues

3\. \*\*Corrective Actions\*\*

\- Document and track corrective measures

\- Link issues to resolution steps

\## Inventory Integration

1\. \*\*Consumables Tracking\*\*

\- Real-time inventory levels at each workstation

\- Automatic reorder triggers

2\. \*\*Tool and Equipment Check-out\*\*

\- Track usage of shared tools and equipment

\- Maintenance scheduling based on usage

\## Safety and Compliance

1\. \*\*Safety Checklists\*\*

\- Daily/shift safety checks

\- PPE compliance tracking

2\. \*\*Incident Reporting\*\*

\- Easy reporting of safety incidents or near-misses

\- Follow-up action tracking

3\. \*\*Environmental Monitoring\*\*

\- Track environmental conditions (temperature, humidity, air quality)

\- Alerts for out-of-range conditions

\## Analytics and Reporting

1\. \*\*Productivity Analysis\*\*

\- Workstation efficiency trends

\- Comparison across similar workstations

2\. \*\*Quality Trends\*\*

\- Defect rates over time

\- Impact of process changes on quality

3\. \*\*Bottleneck Identification\*\*

\- Identify and analyze workflow bottlenecks

\- Suggestions for process improvements

4\. \*\*Custom Report Builder\*\*

\- Generate reports on workstation performance, quality, etc.

\## Integration Features

1\. \*\*Production Planning Integration\*\*

\- Sync workstation capacity with production schedules

2\. \*\*Machine Integration\*\*

\- Link workstation performance to associated machine data

3\. \*\*Inventory System Integration\*\*

\- Ensure availability of materials and consumables

4\. \*\*HR System Integration\*\*

\- Sync operator assignments with scheduling and payroll

\## Optimization Tools

1\. \*\*Layout Optimization\*\*

\- Analyze and suggest optimal workstation layouts

\- Simulate impact of layout changes

2\. \*\*Process Improvement Suggestions\*\*

\- AI-driven suggestions for workflow improvements

\- Best practice sharing across similar workstations

3\. \*\*Predictive Maintenance\*\*

\- Predict maintenance needs based on usage patterns

\- Schedule preventive maintenance to minimize disruption

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Workstation Configuration\*\*

\- Set up and modify workstation details

\- Configure associated equipment and tools

2\. \*\*User Permissions\*\*

\- Set access levels for workstation control and data viewing

3\. \*\*Alert Configuration\*\*

\- Customize alert thresholds for productivity, quality, and safety

4\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Manage connections with other systems (Machines, Inventory, HR)

This comprehensive Workstations Management interface provides tools for
efficient operation, quality control, and optimization of all
workstations in the coating facility, enabling streamlined workflows and
high-quality output.
