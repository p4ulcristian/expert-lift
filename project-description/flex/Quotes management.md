\# Quotes Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Quote Overview\*\*

\- Total number of quotes

\- Quotes by status (Pending, Accepted, Rejected, Expired)

\- Conversion rate (Quotes to Orders)

\- Total value of open quotes

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Create New Quote

\- View Pending Approvals

\- Generate Quotes Report

3\. \*\*Quote List\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable table with columns:

\- Quote ID

\- Client Name

\- Date Created

\- Expiration Date

\- Total Value

\- Status (Pending, Accepted, Rejected, Expired)

\- Assigned To (Sales Rep)

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Convert to Order, Delete

\## Create/Edit Quote Form

1\. \*\*Client Information\*\*

\- Client selection (with option to create new client)

\- Contact person

\- Billing and shipping address

2\. \*\*Quote Details\*\*

\- Quote ID (auto-generated)

\- Date

\- Expiration date

\- Sales representative

3\. \*\*Line Items\*\*

\- Product/Service selection

\- Quantity

\- Unit price

\- Coating specifications (type, color, thickness)

\- Discount (if applicable)

\- Line total

4\. \*\*Additional Services\*\*

\- Pre-treatment options

\- Post-treatment options

\- Packaging and shipping

5\. \*\*Totals\*\*

\- Subtotal

\- Tax

\- Shipping costs

\- Total

6\. \*\*Terms and Conditions\*\*

\- Payment terms

\- Delivery timeline

\- Warranty information

7\. \*\*Notes\*\*

\- Internal notes

\- Notes visible to client

8\. \*\*Attachments\*\*

\- Option to attach relevant documents (e.g., technical specifications,
product images)

\## Quote Details Page

1\. \*\*Quote Summary\*\*

\- All details from the Create/Edit form

\- Current status

2\. \*\*Status History\*\*

\- Timeline of status changes with dates and user who made the change

3\. \*\*Communication Log\*\*

\- Record of all client communications related to this quote

\- Option to add new communication entries

4\. \*\*Related Documents\*\*

\- Link to related orders (if converted)

\- Attachments

5\. \*\*Action Buttons\*\*

\- Edit Quote

\- Send to Client

\- Mark as Accepted/Rejected

\- Convert to Order

\- Duplicate Quote

\## Quote Approval Workflow

1\. \*\*Approval Rules\*\*

\- Set up rules for quotes that require approval (e.g., above certain
value, specific clients)

2\. \*\*Approval Process\*\*

\- Submit for approval

\- Approve/Reject with comments

\- Track approval status

\## Reporting and Analytics

1\. \*\*Quote Performance Report\*\*

\- Conversion rate over time

\- Average time to accept

\- Top performing products/services

2\. \*\*Sales Rep Performance\*\*

\- Quotes created

\- Conversion rate by sales rep

\- Total value of converted quotes

3\. \*\*Client Analysis\*\*

\- Quotes by client

\- Acceptance rate by client

\- Average quote value by client

4\. \*\*Pricing Analysis\*\*

\- Most quoted items

\- Price sensitivity analysis

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Quote Templates\*\*

\- Create and manage quote templates for different types of jobs

2\. \*\*Auto-Expiration\*\*

\- Set default expiration periods

\- Configure automatic follow-ups for expiring quotes

3\. \*\*Notification Preferences\*\*

\- Set up alerts for new quotes, pending approvals, accepted quotes,
etc.

4\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Configure integration with CRM and Order Management systems

This interface provides a comprehensive way to manage the entire quoting
process, from creation to conversion, with detailed tracking and
analysis capabilities.
