# Inventory Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Inventory Overview\*\*

\- Total inventory value

\- Low stock alerts

\- Upcoming reorder dates

\- Recent transactions

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Add New Item

\- Perform Stock Count

\- Generate Inventory Report

\- Create Purchase Order

3\. \*\*Category Summaries\*\*

\- Masking Equipment

\- PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)

\- Spare Parts

\- Coating Materials

\- Consumables

\## Inventory List

1\. \*\*Searchable and Filterable Table\*\*

\- Columns: Item ID, Name, Category, Current Stock, Unit, Reorder Point,
Last Restocked, Status

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Restock

2\. \*\*Bulk Actions\*\*

\- Update quantities

\- Generate labels

\- Export selected items

\## Item Details

1\. \*\*General Information\*\*

\- Item name and description

\- Category and subcategory

\- SKU/Item code

\- Barcode/QR code

2\. \*\*Stock Information\*\*

\- Current quantity

\- Unit of measurement

\- Reorder point

\- Optimal stock level

\- Location in facility

3\. \*\*Supplier Information\*\*

\- Primary supplier

\- Alternative suppliers

\- Lead time

\- Last purchase price

4\. \*\*Usage Statistics\*\*

\- Average monthly usage

\- Usage trend graph

\- Linked work orders or processes

5\. \*\*Transaction History\*\*

\- Recent stock movements (in/out)

\- Purchase history

\## Category-Specific Features

\### Masking Equipment

1\. \*\*Equipment Type Classification\*\*

\- Tapes, plugs, caps, etc.

2\. \*\*Size and Compatibility\*\*

\- Compatible part types

\- Size ranges

3\. \*\*Usage Tracking\*\*

\- Times used (for reusable items)

\- Disposal tracking (for disposable items)

\### PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)

1\. \*\*PPE Type Classification\*\*

\- Respirators, gloves, suits, etc.

2\. \*\*Size and Fit Information\*\*

\- Available sizes

\- Fit testing records

3\. \*\*Expiration Tracking\*\*

\- Manufacture date

\- Expiration date

\- Alerts for approaching expiration

\### Spare Parts

1\. \*\*Equipment Association\*\*

\- Link to specific machines or equipment

2\. \*\*Criticality Rating\*\*

\- Importance of the part for operations

3\. \*\*Maintenance Schedule\*\*

\- Planned replacement dates

\- Integration with maintenance module

\## Inventory Operations

1\. \*\*Stock Receiving\*\*

\- Record new stock arrivals

\- Quality check integration

\- Update stock levels and average costs

2\. \*\*Stock Transfer\*\*

\- Move stock between locations

\- Transfer history logging

3\. \*\*Stock Count\*\*

\- Scheduled and ad-hoc stock counts

\- Discrepancy resolution

\- Adjustment approvals

4\. \*\*Consumption Tracking\*\*

\- Record usage in production processes

\- Automatic deduction from stock

\## Reordering System

1\. \*\*Automated Reorder Alerts\*\*

\- Based on reorder points and lead times

2\. \*\*Purchase Order Generation\*\*

\- Create POs from low stock alerts

\- Approval workflow for POs

3\. \*\*Vendor Management\*\*

\- Preferred vendor list

\- Performance tracking (delivery times, quality)

\## Reporting and Analytics

1\. \*\*Inventory Valuation Reports\*\*

\- By category, location, etc.

2\. \*\*Stock Movement Analysis\*\*

\- Identify fast-moving and slow-moving items

3\. \*\*Consumption Trends\*\*

\- Analyze usage patterns over time

4\. \*\*Stockout Analysis\*\*

\- Track and analyze stockout incidents

5\. \*\*ABC Analysis\*\*

\- Categorize items based on importance and value

\## Integrations

1\. \*\*Procurement System\*\*

\- Seamless creation of purchase orders

2\. \*\*Accounting System\*\*

\- Sync inventory values with financial records

3\. \*\*Production Planning\*\*

\- Ensure availability of materials for scheduled jobs

4\. \*\*Maintenance Module\*\*

\- Coordinate spare parts availability with maintenance schedules

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Category and Subcategory Management\*\*

\- Create and manage custom categories

2\. \*\*Unit of Measurement\*\*

\- Define and manage units

3\. \*\*Location Management\*\*

\- Define storage locations within the facility

4\. \*\*Reorder Rules\*\*

\- Set global and item-specific reorder rules

5\. \*\*User Permissions\*\*

\- Set access levels for inventory management functions

This interface provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing all
aspects of inventory in the coating facility, ensuring optimal stock
levels, efficient operations, and cost-effective procurement.
