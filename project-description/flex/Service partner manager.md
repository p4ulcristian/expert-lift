\# Service and Retail Partners (SRP) Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Partner Overview\*\*

\- Total number of partners

\- Partners by industry (pie chart)

\- Partner activity level (bar graph)

\- Recent partner registrations

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Add New Partner

\- View Pending Approvals

\- Generate Partner Report

3\. \*\*Partner List\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable table with columns:

\- Partner ID

\- Business Name

\- Industry

\- Location

\- Join Date

\- Activity Level

\- Status (Active/Inactive)

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Deactivate/Activate

\## Partner Details Page

1\. \*\*Basic Information\*\*

\- Business Name

\- Contact Person

\- Email

\- Phone

\- Address

\- Industry

\- Website

2\. \*\*Partnership Details\*\*

\- Partner ID

\- Join Date

\- Partnership Level

\- Service Offerings

\- Commission Rate

3\. \*\*Performance Metrics\*\*

\- Total Orders Processed

\- Revenue Generated

\- Customer Satisfaction Rating

\- Order Completion Rate

4\. \*\*Integration Status\*\*

\- API Key

\- Customizer Integration Status

\- Last Sync Date

5\. \*\*Service Configuration\*\*

\- List of configured services (e.g., disassembly, assembly)

\- Pricing for each service

\- Estimated completion time for each service

\- Option to add/edit/remove services

6\. \*\*Order History\*\*

\- Recent orders placed through this partner

\- Order status and details

7\. \*\*Communication Log\*\*

\- Record of emails, calls, and meetings with the partner

8\. \*\*Document Repository\*\*

\- Partnership agreement

\- Training certificates

\- Any other relevant documents

\## Add/Edit Partner Form

1\. \*\*Business Information\*\*

\- All fields from Basic Information section

\- Industry selection (multi-select for partners serving multiple
industries)

2\. \*\*Service Configuration\*\*

\- Add services offered by the partner

\- Set pricing and estimated completion time for each service

3\. \*\*Integration Setup\*\*

\- Generate API key

\- Provide instructions for customizer integration

\- Test integration functionality

4\. \*\*Partnership Terms\*\*

\- Set commission rate

\- Define partnership level (if applicable)

\- Upload partnership agreement

5\. \*\*Training and Onboarding\*\*

\- Schedule training sessions

\- Track completion of onboarding steps

\## Reporting and Analytics

1\. \*\*Partner Performance Report\*\*

\- Filter by date range, industry, location

\- Compare partners based on various metrics

2\. \*\*Industry Insights\*\*

\- Most active industries

\- Emerging partnership opportunities

3\. \*\*Revenue Analysis\*\*

\- Revenue by partner

\- Revenue by industry

\- Growth trends

4\. \*\*Customer Feedback Analysis\*\*

\- Satisfaction ratings by partner

\- Common feedback themes

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Default Commission Rates\*\*

\- Set default rates by industry or partnership level

2\. \*\*Approval Workflow\*\*

\- Configure approval process for new partners

3\. \*\*Notification Preferences\*\*

\- Set up alerts for new registrations, performance milestones, etc.

4\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Manage API access

\- Configure data sync frequency

This interface provides a comprehensive way to manage Service and Retail
Partners, track their performance, and integrate their services
seamlessly with the coating process.
