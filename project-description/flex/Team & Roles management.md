# Team & Roles Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Team Overview\*\*

\- Total number of employees

\- Employees by department/role

\- Current shift status (who\'s working now)

\- Upcoming time off/leaves

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Add New Employee

\- Create New Role

\- Schedule Shift

\- View Open Positions

3\. \*\*Performance Snapshot\*\*

\- Overall team productivity metrics

\- Training completion rates

\- Upcoming performance reviews

\## Employee Management

1\. \*\*Employee Directory\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable list of all employees

\- Quick view: Name, Role, Department, Contact Info, Status

2\. \*\*Employee Profile\*\*

\- Personal Information

\- Name, contact details, emergency contact

\- Hire date, employment status

\- Role & Department

\- Skills & Certifications

\- Performance History

\- Training Records

\- Time Off Balance & History

\- Documents (contracts, evaluations, etc.)

3\. \*\*Employee Onboarding/Offboarding\*\*

\- Checklist of tasks for new hires/departing employees

\- Track progress of onboarding/offboarding processes

\## Role Management

1\. \*\*Role Directory\*\*

\- List of all roles in the organization

\- Associated responsibilities and required skills

2\. \*\*Role Creator/Editor\*\*

\- Role name and description

\- Required skills and certifications

\- Associated permissions in the coating facility software

\- Reporting structure

3\. \*\*Skill Matrix\*\*

\- Visual representation of skills across the organization

\- Identify skill gaps and training needs

\## Scheduling & Time Management

1\. \*\*Shift Scheduler\*\*

\- Create and manage employee schedules

\- View by day, week, or month

\- Drag-and-drop interface for easy scheduling

2\. \*\*Time Off Management\*\*

\- Request and approve time off

\- View team calendar with scheduled time off

3\. \*\*Time Tracking\*\*

\- Clock in/out functionality

\- Overtime tracking

\- Integration with payroll system

\## Training & Development

1\. \*\*Training Calendar\*\*

\- Schedule and track training sessions

\- Mandatory vs. optional training

2\. \*\*Learning Management System\*\*

\- Host online training materials

\- Track course completion and certifications

3\. \*\*Career Development\*\*

\- Set and track career goals

\- Identify growth opportunities within the organization

\## Performance Management

1\. \*\*Goal Setting & Tracking\*\*

\- Set individual and team goals

\- Align with overall business objectives

2\. \*\*Performance Review System\*\*

\- Schedule and conduct regular performance reviews

\- 360-degree feedback option

3\. \*\*Recognition & Rewards\*\*

\- System for peer and manager recognition

\- Track and manage employee rewards/bonuses

\## Compliance & Safety

1\. \*\*Certification Tracking\*\*

\- Monitor required certifications and their expiration dates

\- Automated reminders for renewal

2\. \*\*Safety Training\*\*

\- Schedule and track completion of safety training

\- Incident reporting and tracking

\## Recruitment

1\. \*\*Job Posting Management\*\*

\- Create and publish job openings

\- Track applicants and application status

2\. \*\*Interview Scheduler\*\*

\- Coordinate and schedule interviews

\- Collect and manage interviewer feedback

\## Reporting & Analytics

1\. \*\*HR Dashboard\*\*

\- Key HR metrics (turnover rate, time to hire, etc.)

\- Custom report builder

2\. \*\*Workforce Analytics\*\*

\- Analyze trends in performance, skills, and demographics

\- Predictive analytics for workforce planning

\## Communication & Collaboration

1\. \*\*Team Announcements\*\*

\- Post and manage company-wide or team-specific announcements

2\. \*\*Employee Directory\*\*

\- Searchable directory with contact info and roles

3\. \*\*Team Collaboration Tools\*\*

\- Project management integration

\- Team chat functionality

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Org Chart\*\*

\- Visual representation of the organization structure

\- Easy-to-use tool for updating reporting relationships

2\. \*\*Permissions Management\*\*

\- Set access levels for different roles within the HR system

3\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Connect with other systems (payroll, accounting, etc.)

4\. \*\*Customization Options\*\*

\- Customize fields, forms, and workflows to fit your organization

This interface provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing all
aspects of the team and roles within the coating facility, from hiring
and onboarding to performance management and career development.
