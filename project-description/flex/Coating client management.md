# Coating Clients Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Client Overview\*\*

\- Total number of clients

\- New clients this month

\- Active clients (placed an order in the last 3 months)

\- Client retention rate

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Add New Client

\- View Pending Quotes

\- Generate Client Report

3\. \*\*Client List\*\*

\- Searchable and filterable table with columns:

\- Client ID

\- Company Name

\- Primary Contact

\- Industry

\- Location

\- Last Order Date

\- Total Orders

\- Lifetime Value

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Create Order

\## Client Details Page

1\. \*\*Basic Information\*\*

\- Company Name

\- Primary Contact Name

\- Email

\- Phone

\- Billing Address

\- Shipping Address(es)

\- Industry

\- Website

2\. \*\*Client Statistics\*\*

\- Client Since (date)

\- Total Orders

\- Total Revenue

\- Average Order Value

\- Preferred Coating Types

3\. \*\*Order History\*\*

\- Table of all orders with details like Order ID, Date, Status, Total
Amount

\- Option to view full order details or create a similar order

4\. \*\*Quotes\*\*

\- List of all quotes (pending, accepted, rejected)

\- Option to convert quote to order

5\. \*\*Communication Log\*\*

\- Record of emails, calls, and meetings with the client

\- Option to add new communication entries

6\. \*\*Custom Pricing\*\*

\- Any special pricing arrangements or discounts

\- Volume-based pricing tiers

7\. \*\*Technical Requirements\*\*

\- Specific coating requirements or preferences

\- Any client-specific processes or handling instructions

8\. \*\*Document Repository\*\*

\- Contracts

\- Technical specifications

\- Quality certificates

9\. \*\*Financial Information\*\*

\- Payment terms

\- Credit limit

\- Outstanding balance

\## Add/Edit Client Form

1\. \*\*Company Information\*\*

\- All fields from Basic Information section

\- Option to add multiple contacts

2\. \*\*Billing and Shipping\*\*

\- Billing address

\- Option to add multiple shipping addresses

3\. \*\*Industry and Preferences\*\*

\- Industry selection

\- Preferred coating types

\- Communication preferences

4\. \*\*Custom Pricing Setup\*\*

\- Option to set client-specific pricing or discounts

5\. \*\*Technical Requirements\*\*

\- Fields for specific coating requirements or handling instructions

6\. \*\*Financial Setup\*\*

\- Payment terms

\- Credit limit

\## Client Orders

1\. \*\*Create New Order\*\*

\- Pre-filled client information

\- Product/part selection

\- Coating specifications

\- Quantity and pricing

\- Special instructions

2\. \*\*Bulk Order Upload\*\*

\- Option to upload CSV or Excel file for bulk orders

3\. \*\*Recurring Orders\*\*

\- Set up automated recurring orders for regular clients

\## Reporting and Analytics

1\. \*\*Client Performance Report\*\*

\- Order frequency

\- Revenue trends

\- Product/coating type preferences

2\. \*\*Client Segmentation\*\*

\- Segment clients based on industry, order volume, or lifetime value

\- Targeted marketing suggestions for each segment

3\. \*\*Client Satisfaction Analysis\*\*

\- Survey results

\- Feedback trends

4\. \*\*Revenue Analysis\*\*

\- Revenue by client

\- Revenue by industry

\- Growth trends

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Default Client Settings\*\*

\- Set default payment terms, credit limits, etc.

2\. \*\*Client Categories\*\*

\- Create and manage client categories (e.g., VIP, Regular, New)

3\. \*\*Notification Preferences\*\*

\- Set up alerts for new clients, large orders, overdue payments, etc.

4\. \*\*Custom Fields\*\*

\- Add custom fields to client profiles as needed

This interface provides a comprehensive way to manage coating clients,
track their orders and preferences, and analyze their performance over
time.
