# Recipes Management

\## Main Dashboard

1\. \*\*Recipe Overview\*\*

\- Total number of recipes

\- Most frequently used recipes

\- Recently modified recipes

\- Quality performance of recipes

2\. \*\*Quick Actions\*\*

\- Create New Recipe

\- Duplicate Existing Recipe

\- Generate Recipe Report

3\. \*\*Alert Center\*\*

\- Quality issues related to specific recipes

\- Inventory alerts for recipe components

\- Compliance updates affecting recipes

\## Recipe List

1\. \*\*Searchable and Filterable Table\*\*

\- Columns: Recipe ID, Name, Type of Coating, Created Date, Last Used,
Success Rate, Version

\- Quick action buttons: View Details, Edit, Archive, Apply to Job

2\. \*\*Recipe Categories\*\*

\- By coating type (e.g., Powder, Liquid, E-coat)

\- By application (e.g., Automotive, Industrial, Consumer goods)

\- By substrate material

\## Recipe Details Page

1\. \*\*General Information\*\*

\- Recipe name and ID

\- Creator and last modifier

\- Creation and last modification dates

\- Version history

2\. \*\*Coating Specifications\*\*

\- Type of coating

\- Color specifications (including color codes)

\- Gloss level

\- Texture

3\. \*\*Ingredients\*\*

\- Detailed list of components with exact quantities

\- Links to inventory for each component

\- Substitution options for components

4\. \*\*Application Parameters\*\*

\- Pre-treatment requirements

\- Application method (e.g., spray, dip, electrostatic)

\- Temperature and humidity requirements

\- Curing time and temperature

5\. \*\*Equipment Settings\*\*

\- Specific machine settings required

\- Booth setup instructions

\- Gun settings (for spray applications)

6\. \*\*Quality Standards\*\*

\- Target thickness range

\- Adhesion requirements

\- Hardness specifications

\- Chemical resistance standards

7\. \*\*Safety Information\*\*

\- Required PPE

\- Handling precautions

\- Environmental considerations

8\. \*\*Performance Metrics\*\*

\- Historical success rate

\- Common defects encountered

\- Customer satisfaction data

\## Recipe Creation and Editing

1\. \*\*Recipe Builder\*\*

\- Step-by-step guide for creating new recipes

\- Duplication option for creating variants

2\. \*\*Version Control\*\*

\- Track all changes made to recipes

\- Option to revert to previous versions

\- Comparison tool for different versions

3\. \*\*Approval Workflow\*\*

\- Submit new or modified recipes for approval

\- Multi-level approval process (e.g., lab, production, quality control)

\## Testing and Validation

1\. \*\*Lab Testing Module\*\*

\- Record and track lab test results

\- Comparison with quality standards

2\. \*\*Production Trial\*\*

\- Schedule and track production trial runs

\- Collect and analyze trial data

3\. \*\*Customer Approval Process\*\*

\- Manage customer sample approvals

\- Track customer feedback and requirements

\## Quality Control Integration

1\. \*\*Defect Analysis\*\*

\- Link quality issues to specific recipe parameters

\- Trend analysis of defects by recipe

2\. \*\*Continuous Improvement\*\*

\- Suggest recipe modifications based on quality data

\- A/B testing of recipe variations

\## Inventory Integration

1\. \*\*Component Availability\*\*

\- Real-time check of ingredient availability

\- Forecast ingredient needs based on scheduled jobs

2\. \*\*Alternative Suggestions\*\*

\- Suggest recipe alternatives based on inventory

3\. \*\*Automated Purchasing\*\*

\- Trigger purchase orders for low-stock ingredients

\## Production Integration

1\. \*\*Job Scheduling\*\*

\- Link recipes to production schedule

\- Optimize scheduling based on recipe compatibility

2\. \*\*Real-time Adjustments\*\*

\- Ability to make controlled adjustments during production

\- Log and track all in-process modifications

\## Compliance and Documentation

1\. \*\*Regulatory Compliance\*\*

\- Track recipe compliance with industry standards (e.g., AAMA,
Qualicoat)

\- Manage Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS)

2\. \*\*Customer Specifications\*\*

\- Link customer-specific requirements to recipes

\- Manage customer approvals and certifications

3\. \*\*Audit Trail\*\*

\- Detailed log of all recipe accesses and modifications

\- Generate compliance reports for audits

\## Analytics and Reporting

1\. \*\*Recipe Performance Analysis\*\*

\- Success rates and defect rates by recipe

\- Cost analysis per recipe

2\. \*\*Usage Statistics\*\*

\- Track most and least used recipes

\- Seasonal trends in recipe usage

3\. \*\*Custom Report Builder\*\*

\- Generate detailed reports on recipe performance, usage, etc.

\## Knowledge Management

1\. \*\*Best Practices Library\*\*

\- Document and share successful techniques

\- Troubleshooting guides for common issues

2\. \*\*Training Materials\*\*

\- Create and manage training content for each recipe

\- Track operator certifications for complex recipes

\## Settings

1\. \*\*Units of Measurement\*\*

\- Configure preferred units for different parameters

2\. \*\*User Permissions\*\*

\- Set access levels for viewing, editing, and applying recipes

3\. \*\*Notification Preferences\*\*

\- Configure alerts for recipe-related events (e.g., modifications,
quality issues)

4\. \*\*Integration Settings\*\*

\- Manage connections with other systems (Inventory, Production, Quality
Control)

This comprehensive Recipes Management interface provides tools for
creating, managing, and optimizing coating recipes, ensuring consistent
quality and efficiency in the coating process.
